Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Welcoming Your First Baby – This name was chosen because it clearly reflects the supportive purpose of the site. It resonates with first-time parents preparing for their child’s arrival and is easy to remember.

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub of resources for first-time parents, including preparation checklists, hospital bag planners, health and safety tips, and community advice. It aims to reduce stress and provide clarity during the transition to parenthood.

Scenarios

  • “What items should I pack in my hospital bag?”
  • “How do I baby-proof my home before the baby arrives?”

Color Scheme

Primary: Deep Baby Blue – used for headings and buttons.

Secondary: Pastel Pink – used for highlights and accents.

Neutral: Near Black – used for body text.

Accent: Golden Yellow – used for callouts and emphasis.

Typography

Poppins – used for headings and navigation (modern, approachable).

Roboto – used for body text (clean, readable).

Wireframe

Home Page Layout:

  • Mobile View: Header with site title and hamburger menu → Hero section with welcome message → Quick links (Checklist, Hospital Bag, Health & Safety, Community) → Featured tip card → Footer with contact/support.
  • Desktop View: Header with horizontal navigation → Hero banner with welcome message → Grid layout of quick links → Featured tip section → Footer with contact/support.
