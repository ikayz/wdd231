Health & Safety

Essential guides to keep your little one safe and healthy.

Safe Sleep Practices

Always place your baby on their back to sleep. Use a firm sleep surface covered by a fitted sheet. Keep soft bedding, crib bumpers, and toys out of the crib.

Baby Proofing 101

Start crawling around your home to see it from a baby's perspective. Cover outlets, secure furniture to walls, and place safety gates at stairs.

Nutrition Basics

Whether breastfeeding or formula feeding, ensure your baby is fed on demand. Consult your pediatrician about Vitamin D supplements.

Car Seat Safety

Ensure your car seat is installed correctly. Keep your baby rear-facing as long as possible, according to the seat manufacturer's height and weight limits.
